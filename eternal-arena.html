HOW TO PLAY

🎮 CONTROLS

A - Move Left
D - Move Right
J - Quick Attack
K - Heavy Attack
L - Magic Attack
F - Ultimate (When Charged)

⚡ GAME OBJECTIVE

Defeat your opponent by reducing their health to zero within 5 minutes!

📊 CHARACTER CLASSES

Knights: High health, balanced damage

Assassins: Fast, high critical hits

Mages: High magic damage, low health

💡 TIPS

• Build your Ultimate meter by attacking

• Combo attacks deal more damage

• Each character has unique abilities

• Higher difficulty = stronger opponent
